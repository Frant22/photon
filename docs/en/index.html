Most of your PPC budget is likely being wasted right now

The tough truth: the overwhelming majority of PPC campaigns dramatically underdeliver. This usually translates to you paying far more than necessary for every lead and sale. In an industry overflowing with self-styled experts, it's difficult to find someone you can truly rely on to meaningfully improve your ROI.

"So what can you actually do?"

option 1
Handling PPC internally

When large sums are going into advertising, it's natural to consider managing the campaigns in-house

Lower upfront cost - You save money immediately and keep full control over priorities.

Expertise is critical - PPC is technically demanding, and advertising platforms are built to encourage continuous higher spend.

Huge time demand - Your time has real value – should it be invested in learning complex ad systems or in developing your core business?

option 2
Working with a full-service digital agency

Many full-service agencies will encourage you to include PPC in their overall package

Almost no effort required - One partner manages everything; no need to search for specialists.

Marketing focus vs data focus - Most agencies excel at creative and branding work rather than relentless, numbers-driven ROI optimisation.

Shallow specialisation - Achieving the lowest cost per lead and highest conversion value usually requires deep, specialised knowledge that broad agencies rarely have.

option 3
Employing a dedicated in-house PPC specialist

You can always closely monitor your in-house PPC specialist – could this be a stronger choice?

Strong alignment - Your own employee is naturally motivated to prioritise company success.

High long-term cost - Salary, taxes, benefits, office costs, training and management overhead make this one of the most expensive routes.

Major risk - If performance is below expectations, revenue and sales suffer quietly – often for months before the issue becomes obvious.
