The majority of your PPC ad budget may be going to waste

The sad truth is that most PPC campaigns severely underperform. Meaning that you’re probably already paying too much for your leads and sales. But in an industry where everyone is an overnight ‘expert’, it’s hard to know who you can trust to improve your ROI.

“So what are my options?”

option 01
Managing your PPC account yourself

When spending so much on ads, it’s tempting to try and manage your account internally. At least you can trust your own intentions, right? But this strategy often backfires.

Cheap - You can save some money upfront. And you can trust your own intentions.

Experience is essential - PPC management is complex and intricate, and ad platforms are incentivized to make you spend more and more.

Time intensive - your time is valuable - is it better spent on becoming a PPC expert or on what you do best - running your business?

option 02
Hiring a digital marketing agency

Full-service marketing agencies will try to convince you to let them take care of your PPC campaigns too. But can generalists really make a tangible difference?

Low effort - No need to search for the ideal solution, a full-service agency just handles everything.

Marketing vs data - Most agencies are focused much more on marketing than on a data-driven, ROI maximizing approach.

Lack of expertise - Getting the best value for your ad spend often requires uncommon insider knowledge that only experienced specialists have.

option 03
Hiring an in-house PPC manager

You can always keep tabs on an in-house PPC manager - maybe that’s a better bet?

Trustworthy - As your employee, you can usually trust that they have your best interests at heart.

Expensive option - A recurring salary, in-house overheads, a high termination costs and the need for active management means this is a very costly option for most.

A risky gamble - If your PPC manager is underperforming, not only will they be costing you in lost revenue but you may never even know.
